Hand gesture recognition is a technology that enables a computer to interpret and understand gestures made by a person using their hands. It plays a crucial role in enhancing human-computer interaction, particularly in scenarios where physical touch or traditional input methods may be impractical or cumbersome. The process typically begins with capturing images or video feeds through cameras, followed by preprocessing to improve data quality.

The system then proceeds to identify and isolate hand regions within the captured data, a step known as hand detection.
